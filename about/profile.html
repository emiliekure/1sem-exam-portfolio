Creative and nerdy, all at once. I wear my nerdiness as a badge of honour. To me, it signifies that I am passionate about something, and there is nothing negative associated with that. Besides that I love being creative and crafting visually pleasing projects.

Never really fitting in. I have spent a great deal of my life wondering what I wanted to do when I "grew up". And let me tell you, it did not get any less frustrating as I actually grew up, but didn't get any closer to figuring out my "purpose" in life.

I finally found my calling. I always knew I wanted to work with something that I really love doing. Cut to me sitting down and actually laying out what my main traits and interests were, and discovering that an AP degree in Multimedia Design would allow me to be creatively nerdy as my job.

Fulfilling my purpose. After starting this programme I feel like a completely different person. I truly believe that I am doing what I was always meant to be doing, and I am looking forward to create exciting, creative and nerdy projects for clients in the future.
