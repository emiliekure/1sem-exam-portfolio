Emilie Kure

Multimedia Design student

Hello, my name is Emilie. I am currently studying to become a multimedia designer at Cphbusiness in Lyngby, north of Copenhagen in Denmark.

My goal is to work freelance, and be able to use everything I have learnt and everything I still have yet to learn, in my daily work.

I love what I am learning. During my AP degree, I have so far worked with a good portion of the Adobe Crative Cloud programs, as well as coding in HTML5 and CSS3. Until this point I have gained the most experience in coding, but I find Illustrator and XD very useful and versatile for my ideas and projects, and from what I have seen created in InDesign, I am very excited to work with that as well.

Growing and evolving. Although I learn a lot from my classes, I take a lot of pride in excelling at what I do, and multimedia design is no exception. In order to feel accomplished with doing my best, I will also develop my skills in for example Illustrator and Premiere Pro much further, to provide a well rounded portfolio of services in the future.

There is much more to come. I am beyond excited to continue with my studies in the AP degree, where I am looking forward to working with design thinking, WordPress, InDesign and JavaScript, among other things.

My current plan is furthermore to apply to the top-up bachelor's degree in Web Development, which is also taught at Cphbusiness, once I graduate from Multimedia Design.
