Flow 1 project

Scope

For this project, we were tasked with developing a prototype of our pending portfolio website, in Adobe XD. Additionally, we had to create a short document, where we described and showcased our prototype.

Full project description

Initial idea

I wanted to keep my prototype simple, with corresponding shapes and colors, so I used green, grey and circles throughout, as well as just one font family. I wanted to have a picture as my banner in the header and since my main colour is green, I found a picture of some leaves on a tree.

Challenges and process

I struggled a lot with coming up with content for my front page, but eventually I decided to fill it with bigger buttons for the subpages than the ones in the menu. The banner picture was a bit too vibrant in its colours for my vision, so I decreased the opacity. This also made it easier to read my name on the banner. Again to keep the feel of the site light and airy I created hover effects on the small preview pictures of my projects, in which the opacity increased on hover.

Feedback

My font sizes were a bit large, which was a result of not testing the prototype properly, as well as it being my first time working with Adobe XD. On my contact page I had listed all my socials as well as my personal e-mail, but we were told that a contact form would be better, so our e-mails wouldn't be listed for public use. Like my font was a bit too big, the pictures on my project page were very large as well.
