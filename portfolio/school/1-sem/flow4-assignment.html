Icons in Illustrator

Flow 4 assignment

Scope

We had to create 3-5 web icons that were thematically cohesive, but with no outer requirements to the motifs from our professors.

Full assignment description

Initial idea

I love colours and thought that I could use a lot of different ones if my icons were based on the four seasons. This is also a starting point that really lends itself to simple motifs with clear cut and unambiguous connections to each season.

Challenges and process

To keep the icons simple, all four are made up of two main elements each and every line is rounded at the ends. I based my colour choices on a square on the colour wheel and used red for fall, blue for winter, green for spring and yellow for summer. To distinguish the two elements of each icon and also add some dimension, I used a dark and and a light nuance of the colours. I wanted to make the icons symmetrical, and as it was only my second time using Illustrator, it took some time getting used to elements not aligning with pixels, especially after working with XD.
