Photoshop

Flow 1 assignment

Scope

During our introductory week, we went on an excursion to Copenhagen, where the objective was to take pictures of bridges of different types and interpretations. Back at class we then had to choose one image picturing a bridge, and edit it in Photoshop.

Full assignment description

Initial idea

My first idea was to replace the sky with a sunset sky, because the sunset would give an opportunity to adjust the colors, to match the sky. Second I wanted to clean up the look by removing the graffiti on the side of the bridge. I also wanted to edit in a plant on the supporting ledge as the element to blend in with the rest of the photo. Lastly I had to crop the photo and make sure the format fit 4:3, and my approach was to make less of the water visible and enhance the focus on the sky.

Challenges and process

The replacement of the sky was very straightforward, only the overhead cables required a bit more specific selection to delete. The graffiti on the bridge took a bit longer to remove using auto-fill in selections, because the bars on the railing would occasionally be replicated down. The plant proved too much of challenge for me to add because of the shadowing, as well as blending it in to look natural. I considered adding some moss to the bottom of the plant to make the blending easier, but it was still too advanced for me. With the color adjustments I focused on increasing the red and purple hues to match the new sky.
